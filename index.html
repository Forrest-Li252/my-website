Blast Disk Recovery System
IN USE
CO₂-driven main parachute separation mechanism for Duke AERO high-power rocketry. Previous system suffered unreliable separations from excessive pressurized volume. Custom HDPE sliding disk reduces pressurized volume 5×+, converting CO₂ expansion into shear pin force more efficiently. Integrates sealed cable gland (shock cord) and breakaway VGA connector (avionics wiring). FEA validated at 60 psi — factor of safety 1.95. Machined from 6″ OD HDPE stock.
